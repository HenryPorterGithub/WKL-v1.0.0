The Quiet, Simple Life


Often, when I am low on meds
I have a bit of a verbal tic.
I can rage with the best of them.
Sometimes I am quite horrified
By the scorched earth these tics
Seem to leave, as if I draw on the
Psyches of those near and dear
With charcoal of my own making.
Process art, and it is never kind and
gentle and loving and wrapped up
Like a box of chocolates. It is
Often perceived as full on war fare.
Fear and loathing does that to me.
I allow my emotions to get on the
War path. That is one of the
Benefits of SSRI's, they increase
Your emotional IQ and extend time
In order to give me that counting moment
Where you are supposed to take a deep breath
and wait before you launch in to an
Atomic detonation. I am amazed at
People who use strategy and serve revenge cold.
That has never ever been my style even
Though I admire it in others. I am a hothead.
I can be mean and cruel. When I wait, my rage
only builds to boil over scalding myself as
Well. My emotional intelligence on medication
Enables me to see the nuances of others reactions
Leaving me to actually often take no offence.
Feelings, hurt feelings, bad feelings, these are
My triggers that produce my tics. Emotional
Turrets. My tics are not random outbursts of
Forbidden words. My tics are sharply honed
to inflict maximum damage and are site specific
for vital organs and the jugular. These tics
are designed to make an Opponent bleed
out and I am not after a slow bleed. No
I am after a gusher, a fountain spray of
Lifeblood so I know for sure I have a hit.
I am not at all happy about my tics. I
Never think of them as an asset. I think
they are some vestigial expression
my ancient ancestors needed in order to survive.
What earthly good would these verbal missiles
have been to a hunter gatherer? Why would
early man have needed verbal sparring skills?
No mastodon was stopped in its tracks by
A scathing reference to its moral character.
Mid evil knights were not in the habit of
Using verbal criticism to win a joust.
Perhaps in a cave, in order to ramp up ones
rank, a rant about a rivals poor loyalty
Could accelerate a rise. During a barter
session perhaps you could intimidate in
Order to get a better deal and to warn against
Shady crooked behaviour. Who knows. More
Often that not a mouth like mine would
probably get you killed. I would have died from a
burst appendix in ancient times and without the help
of forceps I would surely have died in childbirth.
So why do I come with a temper and a mind full
Of tricks and tics? I have no idea. I know that no act
of will on my part can stem the tide of my anger
Nor the outburst of verbal tics. Like a tantrum or an
orgasm there is no stopping the train that
Has left the station. My tics act like sentient beings
with a mind of their own. Within me, part of me,
yet set apart and travelling on a set of tracks
That veer off beyond my peripheral vision to
Attack and cause maximum damage. They are
Like a nuclear Missile programmed with a set
Of coordinates. Like lightening these tics
Are formed and fired. Almost instantaneous.
My mind finds the soft underbelly and
formulates a series of words, phrases, concepts,
Denigrations, insults, criticisms,
hurtful observations, lethal confirmations
Seriously hateful substantiation of all that
Is wrong with the other. All that has ever been wrong
with the other and all that is ever likely to be
Wrong in the future. There is always a litany
And a detailed history of every single transgression
Word or deed, thought, imagined or acted out.
Tics are specific, never sporadic.


Everything I know about my world
Internal and external is provisional
