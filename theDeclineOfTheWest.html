The Decline of The West


America sits in his own shit
Breathing air that is worse than
Third world smog
He bullies and harasses
And scares the living daylights
Out of me and many others.
He is self righteous and
Conceited and very dangerous.
Viscousness is his calling card.
Sabotage and subterfuge are currency.
Spontaneous eruptions of violence
Are acts of wonder with names
Like desert storm. Homeland pollution
And fracking and crumbling infrastructure,
Support decline with a war on drugs
And a war on crime.


As portions of society accept change
And learn respect, the dreaded pull
From behind gains traction, resentment
Seethes and roils within the heartland.
Worldwide, competitor nations fail to
Smother their glee. Emboldened they
Begin to speak out against him. Quietly
At first, behind his back, then with boldness
To his face.


America regards his own decline
And squirms with resentment, lashing
Out in anger. Without a game plan how
Can he ever regain his supremacy?
With any conceivable game plan
How can that rubble pile be mounted?
All empires fall and are usurped, I
Live in times of decline, a witness.
