Wrinkled Skin


I am an old woman now
I live in wrinkled skin and failing
Systems and organs grown old with time
And overuse. I am no coward but
I wonder how long I will have
Possession of this body of mine.
At times I have been brave and lived
with ferocity. At other times I have slunk
Off to cower in numb depression. I have
Loved and I have hated. I have tried and
I have often failed. I have travelled and I have
read and I have learned so many things
And I have made art and planted many a
Flowering thing. I have appreciated art and
literature and music and enjoyed the
Company of so many interesting people.
I have enjoyed the company of many
Creatures, dogs and cats and horses and birds
And I have marvelled at the beauty of nature
And the hideous qualities displayed by
Human nature. I believe we are a despicable
Species. When we are gone, shrugged off
the planet like an annoying flea, by our own
Devices, we will probably not be missed.
Our own actions have led us to
The brink of destruction. Our future is bleak.
My future is bleak. I am near the end. What a time
To be alive, what a time to die.
I am an old woman now.
I live in wrinkled skin.
