A Basement of Despair


Lost and pained
What have I gained.
Where have I been
What have I seen
I carry shame
I live with blame
Angry about the game


Angry about injustice
Looking through my accomplice
Affected by all things said and done
Affected by what has been left undone
I see what led me here
Loving the things I hold dear
Morning the loss of what I fear


I wanted an equal partnership
Thinking I'd use intelligence to slip
Along through life avoiding ruts
Living with brains using my guts
But the path I trod was a maze at best
Disappointment, regret, needing rest
I am at the end of this lifelong test.


What is the point of all this try
I try to figure it out but why
Are we here at all in this way
Living a life with no real say
Where are we going, why are we here
Living lives with our vision unclear
Wondering what is the point I sneer
At the mess of this world so queer
