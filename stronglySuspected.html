Strongly Suspected


The anatomy of a long
Marriage, two children,
many jobs, many homes,
Many moves, many times
Miss connected


Loyalties, disloyalties,
Arguments, dissatisfactions
Cheating, lies, theft and stolen
The patterns of our life
Dissected


Arguments, positions, beliefs
Loyalties, women, booze and
Blind ego, justified actions that
I go over, memory intact
Ever corrected


My feelings, my fear, my sadness
Fuels feelings that lead to the black
Dog, depression, antidepressants
Help me cope but constantly I feel
Disrespected


You thought I didn't know, couldn't know
Wouldn't know the truth of your cheating
Heart. But eagle eyed I knew the truth
Always strongly suspected, never
Undetected


I forged ahead each time, I hurt, but I thought
I had my place despite the knowledge of
Your disrespect. But I stood on quicksand
All the time. Knowing the cold truth I was truly
Neglected


In your righteous indignation you choose to
Demonise my part. An old family trick you
Use my depression to falsely claim I am insane.
You paint a false picture of me. I am unfairly
Depicted


The things you have said and done leave me
Reeling and feeling down on myself. Now I
Know where to park my resentment. I know that
You chose to act in so many ways that I truly
Resented


So now we are at that place so long avoided
Where we make plans and try to move on in
Ways not yet known. Not the end we envisioned
A plan for an old age apart and estranged
Implemented


You still play at management and from afar
You intrude and meddle and vie for my attention
Trying to do things that you think will make a
Difference. Never the things I long for, the
Tender loving generosity I yearn for, just
The bossy, bully, pissing in the corners
That leaves me struggling with feelings of
Anger. Railing against the injustice I feel as
Though you will never let me have my say,
My way. Your behaviour is merely
Disingenuous
