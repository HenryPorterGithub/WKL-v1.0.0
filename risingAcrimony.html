Toxic Sludge


How do we handle
The emotional detritus
Of a wonky mind. Humanity
Is perverse in the ways of
Disposal. In third world
Countries the amount of
garbage distributed endlessly
across the landscape and
Occupying every corner was
Enough to kick start an epic
OCD episode for me. Where
Do I park my waste materials?
I have no flush mechanism, no
Recycling centre. I am sick and
Tired of eating that nasty emotional
Sandwich. If it is possible, with
Pharmaceutical help, to close
The kitchen down, mothball
The factory, and stop production
Altogether, I would not miss my
Rising acrimony, my
Toxic sludge.
