Toxic Secrets


The lies I tell
The truths I don't
Toxic secrets boil up
And an elephant stands
in the room, covered in
Flocked wallpaper. I try
To be truthful, I try not
To hide my dirty laundry
But who really wants
To know discomforting
Revelations! I fabricate
Stories to amuse myself
And hammer home a truth.
I weave fantasies and
Project illusions across
The arc of my life for
Entertainment.
The lies I tell myself
The truths I don't


Fairy Tales
Terrible things happen
In fairy tales, poison apples
And needle sticks, prolonged
Somnambulant sojourns
Too,many short men and
And too many prolonged
Sleeps, women stuck in
Impenetrable towers, manic
Spinning and lost youth.
Breakdowns, breakthroughs
These folk tales are part of
An ancient tradition used
To warn us of awaiting fates
Symbolic and literal, these
Cautionary missives are
Meant to prepare us by
Circumscribing behaviour
And giving fair warning
The horror is that in life
Terrible things happen
Tragedy is commonplace
Human nature is despicable
