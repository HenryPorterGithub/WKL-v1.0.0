    • Wendy Knox-Leet
    A bright vessel
    of dark words
    and darker thoughts

    Wendy Knox-Leet is a retired visual and performance artist who is rediscovering her natural talent for poetry. She has was born in Canada, and has lived every single day of her life in a state of profound elegance.

    Her physical body has spent time in the USA, Italy, India, Cairo, Egypt, Abu Dhabi and Doha. It currently lives in a beautiful countryside slice of Bohemia. Physically and legally, it is part of the UK but in actuality, it is a state of mind.

    She has a BA and MFA from York University, Toronto. She completed a residency at PSI, LIC, NY in the early 80s.

    Her poetry is meant to be spoken, and if you are capable of listening, it will be heard.
