A Basement of Despair


Millimetric changes
Basement sliding


These changes
What are they really but
A blizzard of death.
My DNA adrift
Clouds of exfoliation
When the immflamuation
Wars are raging within
I suffer chills and
burning waves of
Pain engulfing my torso.
Along with this fever is
The black dog of
Depression weighing on
My psyche like a
Monumentally heavy wet blanket
Of despair and self loathing.
The pustulating blisters covering
My palms and heels are the
Physical manifestation of my
Eternal suffering as I literally
Eat myself alive.


Basement sliding and
Millimetric changes.


I am sliding into a basement of despair
I am sliding into the anterior of my days
I view my passing from this work of art
My life, with such obvious decay.
The slow decay of a living organism
The sloughing of the outward mantle
The excretion of death and destruction
Through bleeding pustulesk
Reminds me of the extreme trials
And tribulations of the sainted ones.
They were like ordinary people
Facing the challenges of this
Human play. That is the lesson.
