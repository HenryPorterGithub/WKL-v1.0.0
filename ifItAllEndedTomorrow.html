If It All Ended Tomorrow


If it all ended tomorrow
I wouldn't mind a bit


Realistically speaking
Reality is a shadow of the mind
I think, I feel, I eat and sleep
I am here for such a short stay
Sometimes it feels as though
Time passes interminably slowly,
But in all actuality time speeds
Past in record time.
I have used medications to cope
With this passage of time
They help me process the
Passage. 42 years ago I
Visited Dowth and Knowth.
I rubbed the entrance stone and
Traced the circular paths carved
Into the stone. I gave that rubbing
Away immediately because the
Time I spent on the process
Taught me all I needed to know
To follow the map of my life as
It unfolded before me in time. The
Love I journeyed with, to that knowing,
Has recently been released from this
Time. I am old now and often I long
For oblivion. I have crammed a lot of
Living into this time. Experiential activity
I have loved lost and left, crowed with joy
And cried with loss and pain and hate.
I have been noisy and I have often spoken
In haste. I have acted and reacted with
Supreme ignorance. I have accumulated
An inordinate amount of knowledge but
For a clever girl, I have been incredibly
Stupid and selfish. I have also been
Loyal and fiercely true and loved with
Passion. I have known so many wise
And accomplished individuals. I have
Burned so many bridges. I have inspired
And led many an individual to some
Wild and weird revelatory knowing.
I have pissed off a lot of people. I have
Not led a simple quiet life. If it all
Ended tomorrow I wouldn't mind a bit.
