Troubled Times


When you live in
Troubled times
You think it is an
Anomaly
But it is not
Times are always strange
And troubled
Some times are stranger
Than others but
All times reveal uncomfortable truths
As civilisation marches
Towards an unfurling
Change comes
Ideas and behaviours morph
Human nature has a troubled path
Evil intent, hurt and pain
Fear and loathing
The dark underbelly of
Man's inhumanity to man
Continually rears an ugly maw
There are casualties always
Blood flows and human lives
Are lost.


Men and women of power
The cult of personality
The divisions of religion
Politics and prosperity
Those who are
Rich and poor divide
The limited earthly
Resources unevenly


Men and women of power
Crow with belligerent delight
At their ill gotten gains
Young white American boys
Shoot dead their classmates
Police in America shoot dead
The innocent people of colour
Racism is about
Demonising the other
Divisive measures used
To keep a populace
Muzzled and confused
Obediently suffering
For the propping up
For the support of
Men and women of power.


The power usually resides
With white men of European
Extraction.
Women, people of colour
Children all traditionally
Lead lives with little power
And less wealth. Christians sit
At the apex of power
But power ebbs as we
Learn to disrespect
Age old institutional power
And religion and politics.
As we evolve as a species
We accumulate knowledge
Slowly we limp along
Dragging some
Kicking and screaming
Towards our future.


This bumpy march forward
Takes us through troubled times
Reprobates haul us back
Outliers propel us forward
Songs and art forms serve as
Social signals pointing forward.
Points of light as beacons to
Guide our way forward
Emotional strings and ties
Binding us to our
Collective unknown.
Truths and lies compete
And cajole and mark our
Human narrative.
We make false starts and false
Assumptions and take false
Actions. We hurt each other.
We help each other. Actions
And inactions. The dichotomy
Of life that pushes and pulls
The human race along
The rough terrain we must
Traverse as a species on this
March through time.


Clarity is a rare thing
We are blinkered catching a
Rare brief glimpse of how things
Might be in a far off idealistic
Future. What will be the opposing forces then?
Good and evil
Pain and pleasure
Wealth and poverty
Known and unknown
Real and unreal
Sustainable insubstantial
Knowledge and ignorance
Right and wrong
Just and injustice


If we do, as a species
Manage to find an
Equitable balance where
We can share our limited
Resources and steward our
Home planet, will we have an
Outlet for our baser natural
Impulses?
will we ever be
A species where illness and
Pain and horror and a
Shadow underbelly cease to
Exist?
Would we even want that outcome?
