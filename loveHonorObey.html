Love, Honor, and Obey


Hungry
Angry
Lonely
Tired
Fear of being hungry, angry, lonely and tired
All at the same time
A marriage of betrayals
Cheats and lies
A marriage of disloyalty
Extreme disloyalty
A marriage to a man
So self absorbed
So self referential
I become what
That piece he loves to malign
Betray and disappoint
The alter ego he loves to torture
I become a receptacle for his
Anger, denial, disdain and mistreatment.
I can be denied money for groceries
I can be denied oil for the furnace
I can be denied a working bathroom
I can be denied hot water.
I am hungry, angry, lonely and tired
All of the time
The marriage is a sham, two people
Who do not live together and two people
Who do not even like each other.
He has become intolerable to me
We no longer fit. We are abrasions
Against any good will.
Everything I think or do is simply there
For him to stomp to an untimely death
Smashed thoughts, opinions, aspirations, affiliations
He proceeds with destruction in his heart and
Disdain in his thoughts.
