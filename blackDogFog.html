Black Dog Fog


I shed my skin in great blizzards
Psoriatic snow falls
When I remove my black leggings
they are engulfed in a white overlay
Of my personal DNA.
I am shocked at the density of this veil.
How can it happen to this extent?
Do my cells regenerate at a
Quicker rate or will I
One day have shed this
Entire organ, leaving my
Flesh and blood exposed?
As I retreat within my black dog fog
I wonder at the trail of my very essence
That I cast in great drifts upon
The world, as I strive to remain apart.
My personal conscious not telling
And the uncontrolled expulsion of my DNA
Remains my duplicitous conundrum.
If I revealed my true self more
Would I stop this wholesale broadcast?
No, the die is cast
This autoimmune response
Is well entrenched
The inflammation inside my body
The steady consumption of connective tissue
And the steady flurry of distribution
Marks my path through this black dog fog.
