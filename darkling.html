A Basement of Despair


Black velvet, black smoke
Black mould, black humour,
Black swans, black calla lilies
Black fog, black soot
Black soul, black lives
Black pearl, black courage


Blank courage, blank cheque


Black sorrow, black night
Black face, black slime
Black and blue, black heart
Black jack, black skies
Black ice, black mice


Black clouds, black gold
Black webs, black as coal


Blank horror, black honour
Back handed.


Darkening brooding accumulating gloom
Bravery in the face of a darkling trance
Figuring the steps of the dark dance
Dark emotions in dark corners fuel
The movements of the dance.
