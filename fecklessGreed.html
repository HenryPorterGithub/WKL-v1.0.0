We are the species from hell

The rich oil nations
With individuals richer than gods
Are hell bent on annihilation
Running misogyny and an underclass
Of Muslim workers imported from
Nations who remain oil free,
They employ en mass
Expatriated white secular christians
Men over a certain age
Rogue bull elephants
Who have journeyed to these sun baked sand deserts
To work for triple the pay with no tax
At the ass end of their careers
Lending expertise as relative captives
Unlike brown skinned workers
They can hold their passports
But in order to leave the country
They must have the express permission
of their employers and the local government.
A new kind of indentured servitude
